Introduction

Poppygo

The poppy plant is an herbaceous perennial, often appearing in shades of bright orange to deep scarlet with cup-shaped petals. The papaver orientale (oriental poppy) bloom in mid-summer, and originate from Central Asia. The poppy plant is renowned for its visual beauty and striking color, however, conversely, also for their illicit innards and their hand in the contentious history of 19th century China. The dichotomy of the poppy inspires Poppygo. A colorful corner on your local street; Poppygo boasts delicious Asian snacks and vibrant scenery, while also holding hundreds of unique stories in its shelves, containing everything from sweet romances to sinister mysteries. Find a new favorite story, grab a quick noodle lunch, or both—pick your poison?
